IDBI Bank – Vasundhara Green Deposit

Overview:
IDBI Bank, as part of its commitment towards sustainability and green initiatives, has introduced the “Vasundhara Green Deposit” — a unique term deposit designed to promote eco-friendly and sustainable investment. This product aligns with the Reserve Bank of India’s Green Deposit Framework and allows customers to contribute towards creating a greener future.

Specially designed green deposit receipts are issued to investors as a token of appreciation for their contribution to environmental sustainability.

What is a Green Deposit:
A Green Deposit is a type of term deposit developed under the RBI’s Green Deposit Framework (circular dated April 11, 2023). It provides customers the opportunity to channelize their savings toward environmentally sustainable projects through IDBI Bank’s Vasundhara Green Deposit Scheme. The deposit offers competitive interest rates while ensuring that funds are used exclusively for eco-friendly projects.

How Your Investment Works:
When customers invest in a Green Deposit, their funds are earmarked exclusively for green financing activities as defined by the RBI.  
Eligible project categories include:
- Renewable energy projects  
- Sustainable water management  
- Biodiversity conservation  
- Waste management and recycling  
- Pollution control and prevention  
- Clean transportation and energy efficiency projects  

Reference:
RBI Circular dated April 11, 2023 – “Framework for Acceptance of Green Deposits”.

Salient Features:

Eligibility:
- Individuals  
- Hindu Undivided Families (HUFs)  
- Corporates  
- Trusts  
- NGOs  
- NRE / NRO customers  
- Other eligible entities as per bank policy  

Deposit Type & Amount Limits:
- **Retail Term Deposit (RTD):**
  - Minimum: ₹10,000  
  - Maximum: Less than ₹3.00 crore  
- **Premium Term Deposit (PTD):**
  - Minimum: ₹3.00 crore and above  
  - Maximum: Up to ₹7.50 crore  
- **Bulk Term Deposit (BTD):**
  - Minimum: Above ₹7.50 crore  
  - Maximum: No upper limit  

Deposit Tenure:
- Minimum period: **1111 days**  
- Maximum period: **1111 days**  
(Fixed tenure only)

Rate of Interest:
- As decided by the **Asset Liability Committee (ALCO)** from time to time.  
- Rates are subject to periodic review and revision by the bank.

Additional Interest Benefits:
- Resident senior citizens and bank staff / retired staff senior citizens are eligible for additional interest rates as per the bank’s existing policy.

Key Highlights:
- Investment aligned with RBI’s Green Finance guidelines.
- Contributes to projects supporting environmental sustainability.
- Competitive interest rates with assured returns.
- Exclusive “Green Deposit Certificate” as recognition for participation in green initiatives.
