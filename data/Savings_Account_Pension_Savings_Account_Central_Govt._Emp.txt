IDBI Bank Pension Savings Account

Overview:
The Pension Savings Account is designed especially for pensioners to make banking simple, convenient, and worry-free. 
With this account, you can enjoy banking anywhere and anytime, along with special privileges, faster transactions, 
and a range of value-added services for a hassle-free banking experience.

Features and Benefits:
- **Zero Balance Account:**  
  No need to maintain a minimum balance — enjoy complete freedom from balance requirements.

- **No Charges for Non-Maintenance of MAB:**  
  Pensioners are not penalized for low balances.

- **Free International ATM cum Debit Card:**  
  Access your funds globally with ease.

- **Personalized Multicity Cheque Book:**  
  Convenient cheque transactions across all IDBI Bank branches.

- **Free ATM Transactions:**  
  Get five free ATM transactions every month at other Bank ATMs in Non-Metro locations.

The IDBI Bank Pension Savings Account Advantage:
- Faster fund transfers and online bill/tax payments.  
- Attractive interest rates to grow your savings.  
- Full range of Instant Banking facilities.  
- Free demand drafts and pay orders on request.  
- International Debit Card with global access.  
- Easy and secure digital payments.  
- Value-added services, travel benefits, and gift solutions.
