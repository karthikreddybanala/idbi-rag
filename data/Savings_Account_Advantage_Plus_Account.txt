IDBI Bank Advantage Savings Plus Account

Overview:
The Advantage Savings Plus Account provides complete banking convenience and a financial package to access your money easily. 
IDBI Bank offers a range of savings accounts for optimal money management. With this account, you not only save your money but also make it grow.

Features and Benefits:
- Minimum Average Balance (MAB): Rs. 25,000 across all locations.
- International Debit cum ATM Card.
- Free eight ATM transactions at other Bank ATMs at all locations.
- Passbook facility.

Account Advantages:
- Faster transfer of funds.
- Online options to pay your bills or taxes.
- Opportunities to grow your money at attractive interest rates.
- Instant Banking access.
- International Debit Card usage.
- Demand drafts and pay orders.
- Easy digital payments.
- Access to "Bank on the Move" services.
- Value-added services for better financial management.
- Travel and gift solutions.
