Overview:
The Unnati Business Account is designed to provide flexibility and convenience for business account holders. It allows freedom from maintaining monthly average balance (MAB) initially, supports grouped accounts, and rewards customers for digital transactions.

Product Features:

1. Freedom from MAB
   - No maintenance of monthly average balance (MAB) for the first 3 months

2. Grouping of Accounts
   - Maintain MAB at the family or business level
   - Flexibility and fungibility in MAB maintenance

3. Loyalty Program
   - Earn reward points on account opening
   - Earn reward points on digital transactions

4. Unlimited Free Digital Transactions
   - Free add-on savings accounts with Nil MAB requirement
