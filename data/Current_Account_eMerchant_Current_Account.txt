Overview:
The eMerchant Current Account is designed for merchants to collect card-based payments easily without requiring an EDC machine or website. It provides a simple, mobile and desktop-friendly way to bill customers and track payment status.

Product Features:

1. Payment Requests
   - Merchant can create a payment request with necessary information
   - Customer receives request via email or mobile
   - Customer views invoice and initiates payment via a unique URL

2. Status Tracking
   - Merchant can monitor payment status (paid/unpaid)
   - Resend email/SMS option available
   - Sender email ID is configurable

Benefits to Merchant:

- Bill on the go via mobile or desktop
- Collect card-based payments without an EDC machine or website
- No separate integration required
- Customized payment options
