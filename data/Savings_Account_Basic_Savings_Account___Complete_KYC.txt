IDBI Bank Basic Savings Account (Complete KYC)

Overview:
IDBI Bank presents the Basic Savings Account (Complete KYC) — a zero-balance savings account designed to promote financial inclusion. 
This account is ideal for customers who want to enjoy the convenience of modern banking without maintaining a minimum balance.

Features and Benefits:
- **Free Debit cum ATM Card and Cheque Book:**  
  Get a free ATM cum Debit Card and request a cheque book at no extra cost.  
  The card allows seamless transactions at IDBI Bank ATMs, retail outlets, and online stores.

- **Multiple Access Channels:**  
  Access your account easily through Internet Banking, Phone Banking, or SMS Banking — anytime, anywhere.

- **No Cash Deposit Charges:**  
  Enjoy free cash deposits at any IDBI Bank branch — both home and non-home branches.

The IDBI Bank Basic Savings Account Advantage:
- Zero Balance Account — no minimum balance requirement.
- Free International Debit cum ATM card.
- SMS and Email Alerts for all transactions.
- Free consolidated monthly account statements through email.
