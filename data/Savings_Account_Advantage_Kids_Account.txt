IDBI Bank Advantage Kidz Account

Overview:
IDBI Bank recognizes the importance of helping parents teach their children about savings from an early age. 
With this vision, IDBI Bank has introduced the "Advantage Kidz Account" — a savings account designed specifically to meet the financial needs of children.

This account acts as a modern piggy bank that not only keeps money safe but also earns interest. 
It allows children to withdraw money when required, make smart purchases using an exclusive debit card, 
and learn how to operate their bank account in a secure and convenient way. 
It also helps inculcate financial discipline and awareness among kids.

Through this account, children can start saving the money they receive from parents or guardians. 
These savings will not only build financial awareness but also act as a stepping stone to a more responsible financial future. 
Additionally, children holding this account may later avail of education loans from IDBI Bank at competitive interest rates 
to fund their higher education in India or abroad.

Features and Benefits:
- Minimum Average Balance (MAB): Rs. 5,000
- Exclusive, specially designed Kids ATM Card
- Withdrawal limit: Rs. 5,000 per day at ATM/POS
- Personalized Cheque Book
- Free monthly statements via email on request
- Free Passbook
- Demand Drafts and Pay Orders on request
- Five free ATM transactions at other bank ATMs at Non-Metro locations

Documentation Required:
- ISA of Guardian
- Minor Declaration signed by the Guardian
- Guardian’s photograph and signature on Account Opening Form (AOF)
- Only natural guardians as per law are acceptable. Otherwise, the guardian should be appointed by a court of law.
- Two minors or two guardians are not allowed for a single minor account.
