IDBI Bank Woman Savings Account | Advantage DIVA Savings Account

Overview:
IDBI Bank introduces a special savings account designed exclusively for women — the Advantage DIVA Savings Account. 
This account offers a unique combination of financial benefits, convenience, and special privileges. 
Additionally, women can open a zero-balance savings account absolutely free for their child below 18 years of age.

Features and Benefits:
- Minimum Average Balance (MAB): Rs. 10,000 across all locations.
- One zero-balance savings account for your child below 18 years.
- Specially designed Women’s International ATM cum Debit Card.
- Higher ATM cash withdrawal limit: Rs. 40,000 per day.
- Personalized PAP Cheque Book.
- Up to 50% discount* on locker services.
- Free statement by e-mail.
- Five free transactions on other bank ATMs.

(*Conditions apply)

Account Advantages:
- Free account for children below 18 years.
- Simple documentation for account opening.
- Faster transfer of funds.
- Online options to pay bills or taxes conveniently.
- Secure and convenient digital banking experience.
