IDBI Bank Suvidha Fixed Deposit

Overview:
The IDBI Bank Suvidha Fixed Deposit offers safety, credibility, attractive interest rates, and assured liquidity. It allows customers to earn higher returns on their savings with flexible tenures and convenient access to funds. Interest rates under this scheme are among the most competitive in the industry.

Advantages:
- Anytime access to your fixed deposits.
- Tenure options ranging from 7 days to 20 years.
- Minimum deposit starting from Rs. 10,000.
- Attractive interest rates.
- Senior citizens enjoy an additional 0.50% interest rate on fixed deposits.
- Penalty applicable for premature withdrawal.
- Option to avail overdrafts against deposits at competitive rates.
- Auto-renewal facility available.
- Multiple deposit options to match customer needs.

Features and Deposit Options:

1. **Monthly, Quarterly, and Annual Income Plans**
   - Ideal for customers seeking regular income.
   - Interest credited automatically to your linked savings account at chosen intervals (Monthly / Quarterly / Annual).
   - Monthly payout calculated at a discounted monthly rate; annual payout compounded quarterly.
   - Auto-renewal on maturity ensures uninterrupted interest accrual.
   - Minimum deposit: Rs. 10,000.

2. **Quarterly Compounding Suvidha Fixed Deposit**
   - Best suited for those seeking higher returns with low risk.
   - Interest earned is reinvested every quarter, leading to higher effective yield.
   - Example: For a 2 years + 1 day deposit at 5.80% p.a., effective yield is 6.11% p.a.
   - Auto-renewal ensures continued interest earnings.
   - Minimum deposit: Rs. 10,000.

3. **Sweep-In Savings Facility**
   - Combine the flexibility of a savings account with the higher interest of fixed deposits.
   - Link multiple fixed deposits to your savings account.
   - Automatically sweep funds into the savings account when balance is low (last FD broken first to minimize interest loss).
   - Enjoy liquidity with FD-level returns.

4. **Overdraft Against Suvidha Fixed Deposit**
   - Access instant liquidity without breaking your deposit.
   - Avail overdraft up to 90% of total Suvidha Fixed Deposit value.
   - Eligible for deposits of Rs. 10,000 or higher.
   - Overdraft provided first against deposits earning the least interest to minimize loss.
   - Competitive interest rates on overdraft amount.

5. **Senior Citizen Suvidha Fixed Deposit**
   - Senior citizens earn an additional 0.50% p.a. on deposits of 1 year and above.
   - Tenure options: 7 days to 10 years.
   - Minimum deposit: Rs. 10,000.
   - Available under both regular income and reinvestment plans.
