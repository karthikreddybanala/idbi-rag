IDBI Bank Capital Gain Account Scheme (CGAS), 1988

Overview:
IDBI Bank is authorized by the Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) to accept deposits under the Capital Gains Accounts Scheme (CGAS), 1988, as per Gazette Notification dated November 29, 2012. 
All IDBI Bank branches (except rural branches) can receive deposits and maintain accounts under this scheme.

The Capital Gain Account Scheme facilitates investment of capital gains to save tax on gains earned from selling capital assets such as residential houses, flats, shares, family businesses, farmhouses, or agricultural land.

Eligibility:
All assesses eligible for exemption under Sections 54, 54B, 54D, 54F, 54G, or 54GB of the Income Tax Act, 1961, and who cannot utilize their capital gains before filing their income tax returns, can invest under this scheme to avail tax benefits.

Features and Benefits:

Nature of Facility:
- Acceptance of deposits under CGAS, 1988, either as a Savings Deposit or Term Deposit to claim capital gains tax exemption.

Target Segment:
- Individuals or entities seeking to invest capital gains proceeds to save Capital Gains Tax.

Types of Deposit Accounts:
- **Type A Account (Savings Account):** Regular savings account under CGAS for transactions and withdrawals.
- **Type B Account (Term Deposit):** Fixed-term deposit account under CGAS for long-term investment.

Important Note:
- Customers wishing to maintain only a Type B Account must first open a Type A Account, as all transactions must be routed through Type A.
- NRIs can open deposits under CGAS through the NRO (Non-Resident Ordinary) scheme.
- All account operations will follow CGAS, 1988 guidelines.

Rate of Interest:
- Interest rates applicable are the same as those for standard IDBI Bank Savings and Term Deposits.
- Senior Citizens are eligible for additional interest benefits as per prevailing bank policy.

Tax Deduction at Source (TDS):
- Applicable as per prevailing Income Tax guidelines.

Premature Withdrawal:
- Allowed as per bank policy and CGAS provisions.
- Penalty for premature withdrawal will apply in line with IDBI Bank’s Term Deposit (RTD) premature withdrawal policy.

Utilization of Withdrawn Amount:
- As per CGAS, 1988 guidelines, the withdrawn funds must be utilized strictly for purposes specified under the Income Tax Act.
