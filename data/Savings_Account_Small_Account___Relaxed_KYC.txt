IDBI Bank Small Account (Relaxed KYC)

Overview:
IDBI Bank introduces the Small Account (Relaxed KYC) — a savings account designed to promote inclusive banking. 
This account is ideal for individuals with minimal documentation requirements and offers zero balance facilities. 
It’s simple, convenient, and accessible to everyone.

The IDBI Bank Small Account Advantage:
- Free Debit cum ATM card
- SMS and Email alerts for every transaction
- Free consolidated monthly account statements via email

Features and Benefits:
- **Debit cum ATM Card and Cheque Book:**  
  Get a free ATM cum Debit Card and request a cheque book at no additional cost. 
  Use the card for cash withdrawals, shopping, and online payments.

- **Multiple Access Channels:**  
  Access your account through Phone Banking, SMS Banking, and Internet Banking — anytime, anywhere, without visiting the branch.

- **No Cash Deposit Charges:**  
  Enjoy zero charges for cash deposits at both home and non-home branches.

- **Zero Balance Account:**  
  Maintain financial flexibility with no minimum balance requirement.
