IDBI Bank – Kutumb Family Banking

Overview:
With Kutumb, you can ensure your family gets greater access to finances. The primary family member maintains the required minimum balance on behalf of all members, providing flexibility and convenience.

Features:

- Specially designed accounts for Women, Children, Youth, and Senior Citizens
- Facility to maintain minimum balance by primary member on behalf of entire family
- Greater flexibility to access own funds
- All accounts under one roof

Eligibility:

- Minimum members: 2 | Maximum members: 5
- Eligible family members: Spouse, Mother, Father, Child, Grandparent, Grandchild, Brother, Sister, Father-in-law, Mother-in-law
- Eligible accounts: 
  - All Individual Savings Accounts except Basic Savings Bank Deposit / Financial Inclusion / Capital Gains Accounts
  - All Individual Current Accounts / Sole Proprietor Accounts / HUF
- Exceptions: 
  - Accounts already grouped under another Family ID cannot be grouped again
  - Accounts with Freeze / Negative Balance / Unrecovered charges / Inactive / Dormant accounts are restricted

MAB Requirement:

- Family MAB (FMAB) = sum of MAB of all individual accounts in the group
- FD / FFD balances are excluded for FMAB calculation

Facilities & Charges:

- Account-level facilities and charges as per Schedule of Facilities (SoF) continue unchanged
- Penal charges for non-maintenance of FMAB will be computed as the summation of product-level penal charges
- Recovery of penal charges: from Primary Family Member’s account; if insufficient, then sequentially from other accounts in the group
