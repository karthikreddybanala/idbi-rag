IDBI Bank Advantage Bonanza Account

Overview:
The Advantage Bonanza Account is a premium savings account designed to meet your everyday banking requirements with added convenience and exclusive benefits. 
It offers a complete banking experience with enhanced features, attractive privileges, and value-added services — all tailored for your lifestyle.

This account is ideal for individuals seeking superior banking facilities, higher transaction limits, and special offers on additional services.

Benefits and Features:
- Minimum Average Balance (MAB): Rs. 50,000 across all locations
- Free NEFT transactions through IDBI NetBanking
- Any branch cheque deposit and account-to-account transfers at no extra cost
- Enhanced daily cash withdrawal and POS limits using the International Debit cum ATM Card
- 25% discount on locker charges (any size)
